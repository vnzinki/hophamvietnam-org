## Relevance to Lineage/Genealogy Sites (Dòng Họ)

- **Endless knot** — Symbolizes continuity, interconnection of generations
- **Dong Son drum patterns** — Vietnamese cultural identity, especially Chim Lạc birds
- **Dragon & Phoenix pair** — Union, prosperity, virtue (used in ancestor halls)
- **Crane (hạc)** — Found on Vietnamese ancestor altars, symbolizes longevity
- **Chrysanthemum & Plum blossom** — Longevity, nobility, renewal
- **Chữ Thọ / Song Hỷ / Ngũ Phúc** — Longevity, happiness, and blessings for the family

## Usage Notes

- Most SVGs use minimal colors (monochrome or single color) for easy reuse
- Patterns can be recolored by changing `fill` and `stroke` attributes
- For web use: gold (#C8A84E) on dark backgrounds works well for cultural/historical aesthetic
- CC-BY-SA files require attribution if used publicly
- CC0 and Public Domain files can be used freely without attribution
